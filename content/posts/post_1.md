## _Kali Linux_
_Kali Linux is a Debian-derived Linux distribution designed for digital forensics and penetration testing. It is maintained and funded by Offensive Security._
_Here are 13 reasons why you should use it for penetration testing_

### 1.More Tools
Kali contains several hundred tools which are geared towards various information security tasks, such as Penetration Testing, Security research, Computer Forensics and Reverse Engineering.

### 2.Free
Kali Linux is completely free to use and always will be. You will never, ever have to pay for Kali Linux.
